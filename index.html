About the candidate

Ahmad Saleh

Ahmad is a registered nurse and a medical device executive at Philips Healthcare. He is also the co-founder of a special needs student transportation company he built alongside his mother, growing it from the ground up.

He lives on Bee Meadow Parkway with his multigenerational family, the home he chose so several generations could live under one roof. That is the lens he brings to local government: this is not an abstraction to him, it is his street, his water, his taxes, his neighbors.

“I will research it for hours, talk to the right people, ask questions, and then report back.”

That is the standard he wants to hold the Township Committee to. Do the homework, talk to the people affected, and come back with straight answers.

Where Ahmad stands

Hanover Vision 2036

Government should think beyond the next budget cycle. Hanover Vision 2036 is a long-term strategic planning initiative that brings residents, business owners, first responders, schools, and community organizations together to develop a shared vision for our Township over the next decade, while also prioritizing transparency and accountability. Together, we can make decisions that preserve Hanover’s character and prepare our community for the future.
